Milestone 6: Functional Prototype II

Changes:

Based on our user testing from last week, we realized that we needed to pivot our idea slightly to keep users engaged in the silent-film-making process. We needed a way for users to communicate with more than just a sequence of 2 or 3 videos. Thus, we decided to make our app into a collaborative silent movie making platform (similar to Google Docs, but for silent films). Users can start a thread with one or more people and each person in the thread can make and contribute silent movie clips. When a movie is finished, users can export it and share it with their friends.

Other changes that we made include: showing users by their Facebook name instead of their made-up nickname, reversing the order of the videos (newest on bottom), removing the end title cards from all individual videos (users have the chance to add an ending card to the whole movie when they export), and various UI and design changes throughout the app.



New Features:
  • Start threads with multiple people
  • Option to skip adding a title card in any video
  • Cancel button on the create card and video screens
  • Automatically returns to thread view when movie finishes playing
  • Deletion of a video from its thread
  • Export finished movie to our website
Implementation Progress:

All of the features and changes mentioned above have been implemented. We are currently working on exporting our silent movies directly to Facebook and sending them via email. We will also continue to clean up the UI in the coming weeks. Another one of our goals is to get rid of the the volume bar (which is competely useless) at the bottom of our videos.
